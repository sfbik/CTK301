Starting as my first musical project in 2019, Ahero has been my main passion and focus when it comes to music. Utilizing silky synthesizers and hard-hitting breakbeats, combined with influence from an era and age of technology long gone, Ahero is a chillwave/chillsynth/vaporwave/dreampop project that envelopes the energy of a generation you had yet to live through.

Talk to Me is a side project that started during 2020 as a way to make fun, less-serious, music. Using older synthesizer hardware and software, this project was more focused on loosely-written bedroom pop. With lyrics ranging from wanting to go to space, all the way to being stuck in terrible relationships, Talk to Me is all about having fun in the end.
